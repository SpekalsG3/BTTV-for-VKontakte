Original BTTV

"BTTV for VKontakte addon" provides emote display in messages tab. Maybe there will be more functionality in the future to improve overall VKontakte experience and you can help me at github repository.

BetterTV GIF Emotes — Adds animated emotes (not everyone likes GIFs, but most people do)
Emote Menu — Enables a more advanced emote menu for VKontakte
FrankerFaceZ Emotes — Enables emotes from that other extension people usually use
List of autocompleted emotes — Shows list of emotes that available for autocompletion

This addon does NOT use, store, collect or share any of your personal information except settings provided in this popup's settings tab.
